Our Services

Expert solutions for high-performance buildings.

Green Building Certification

We provide comprehensive facilitation for various green building rating systems. From initial feasibility to final submission, we ensure your project meets all necessary criteria for certification.

  • LEED (Leadership in Energy and Environmental Design)
  • IGBC (Indian Green Building Council) Ratings
  • WELL Building Standard
  • EDGE (Excellence in Design for Greater Efficiencies)

Energy Modeling & Simulation

Our advanced simulation team uses state-of-the-art software to predict building performance, optimize HVAC systems, and ensure daylighting comfort while minimizing glare.

  • Whole Building Energy Simulation
  • Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD)
  • Daylight & Glare Analysis
  • Sun Path & Shading Analysis

Net-Zero & Carbon Advisory

As the world moves towards decarbonization, we help organizations define and achieve ambitious net-zero targets for carbon, energy, water, and waste.

  • Carbon Footprint Assessment
  • Net-Zero Energy Strategies
  • Life Cycle Assessment (LCA)
  • ESG Reporting & Strategy

Water & Resource Optimization

Water is a precious resource. We design integrated water management systems that reduce demand, recycle wastewater, and harvest rainwater.

  • Water Balance Charts
  • Rainwater Harvesting Design
  • STP/ETP Technology Selection
  • Solid Waste Management Plans
