About Us

Building a sustainable legacy, one project at a time.

Our Story

Driving the Green Building Revolution

Founded in 2010, EMYR Green Building Consultants was born out of a passion for sustainability and a vision to reduce the environmental impact of the construction industry. We started as a small team of energy analysts and have grown into a premier consulting firm.

Today, we spearhead projects across the globe, helping developers, architects, and governments create buildings that are not only compliant but exemplary in their performance.

Our Mission

To accelerate the adoption of sustainable building practices by providing world-class technical expertise and innovative solutions that balance economic viability with environmental stewardship.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in green building consultancy, setting new benchmarks for performance, health, and sustainability in the built environment.
